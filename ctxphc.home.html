Welcome to the

Central Texas Parrot Head Club

Where the Heart of Margaritaville Lives On!

The purpose of the Central Texas Parrot Head Club is to provide charitable and educational activities that promote the general welfare of Austin and the Hill Country in Central Texas. Our club is a local 501 (c) nonprofit chapter of the Parrot Heads in Paradise, Inc., whose purpose is to assist in community and environmental concerns and provide a variety of social activities for people who are interested in the music of Jimmy Buffett and the tropical lifestyle he personifies.
